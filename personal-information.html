About Me

I am Ye Kyaw Tun, a dedicated and enthusiastic mechanical engineer. I hold a degree in Mechanical Engineering and have a diverse professional background in various roles.

In my most recent position as a Mechanical Engineer, I have had the opportunity to work closely with supplier teams, ensuring the correct installation of systems and components. I excel at reviewing complex design documents, coordinating testing activities, and troubleshooting issues during commissioning. I take pride in providing training to operators, ensuring the efficient and safe operation of commissioned systems.

Prior to this, I served as an HSE Manager at Myanmar I-Tex Garment factory, where I was responsible for ensuring compliance with health, safety, and environmental regulations. I advised the Safety Committee, reviewed safety specifications for new equipment, and investigated incidents thoroughly, contributing to a safer work environment.

I also worked as a SHEQ Officer at UPG Paints factory, overseeing safety compliance, implementing management systems, and supervising waste disposal.

Additionally, I have experience as a Sales Engineer at Win OSHE Services, where I provided technical documentation, delivered goods, and supported training sessions, enhancing client understanding and satisfaction.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about my diverse experiences. I am enthusiastic about contributing my skills and knowledge to new challenges and eager to be a valuable asset to any team. I look forward to discussing how I can make a meaningful impact in your organization.

About Me

I am Ye Kyaw Tun, a dedicated and enthusiastic mechanical engineer. I hold a degree in Mechanical Engineering and have a diverse professional background in various roles.

In my most recent position as a Mechanical Engineer, I have had the opportunity to work closely with supplier teams, ensuring the correct installation of systems and components. I excel at reviewing complex design documents, coordinating testing activities, and troubleshooting issues during commissioning. I take pride in providing training to operators, ensuring the efficient and safe operation of commissioned systems.

Prior to this, I served as an HSE Manager at Myanmar I-Tex Garment factory, where I was responsible for ensuring compliance with health, safety, and environmental regulations. I advised the Safety Committee, reviewed safety specifications for new equipment, and investigated incidents thoroughly, contributing to a safer work environment.

I also worked as a SHEQ Officer at UPG Paints factory, overseeing safety compliance, implementing management systems, and supervising waste disposal.

Additionally, I have experience as a Sales Engineer at Win OSHE Services, where I provided technical documentation, delivered goods, and supported training sessions, enhancing client understanding and satisfaction.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about my diverse experiences. I am enthusiastic about contributing my skills and knowledge to new challenges and eager to be a valuable asset to any team. I look forward to discussing how I can make a meaningful impact in your organization.

SHEQ Officer

I worked as a SHEQ Officer at UPG Paints Factory from ( January.2017 - November.2018 ). The responsibilities are as follows:

  • Enforced Safety Compliance: Conducted regular inspections to ensure adherence to safety protocols across multiple domains, including Employee Safety, Process Safety, Environmental Safety, and Distribution Safety. Provided oversight to ensure employees and contractors strictly followed safety instructions and regulations.
  • Safety Induction Training: Delivered comprehensive safety inductions to contractors and visitors, ensuring full understanding of safety procedures and protocols before site access.
  • Incident Management and Reporting: Supervised the response to workplace incidents, ensuring injured individuals received timely first aid or hospitalization. Prepared and submitted detailed incident reports to management, contributing to ongoing safety improvements.
  • Health and Medical Coordination:Collaborated with township Health Department to organize periodic medical checkups for employees, promoting a culture of health and safety within the workplace.
  • OHSAS 18001 & ISO 14001 Implementation: Coordinated the implementation of Occupational Health and Safety Management System (OHSAS 18001) and Environmental Management System (ISO 14001), ensuring compliance with international standards.
  • Hazardous Waste Management: Managed the safe disposal of hazardous and non-hazardous waste in compliance with environmental regulations, minimizing workplace and environmental risks.
  • Inspection Reporting: Compiled and submitted detailed weekly and monthly inspection reports, highlighting key findings and recommending corrective actions to maintain high safety standards.
  • Pest Control Oversight: Monitored and assessed the performance of pest control services on a monthly basis, ensuring a safe and hygienic work environment.

HSE Manager

I worked as a HSE Manager at Myanmar I-Tex Garment Factory from ( January.2019 - May.2020 ). The responsibilities are as follows:

  • Responsible for ensuring that the company understands and complies with all laws and regulations regarding health, safety, and the environment.
  • Responsible for health and safety requirements for all inspections such as Government inspector,BSCI ,WRAP ,OEKO-TEX , etc…
  • Acts as an advisor to the Safety Committee.
  • Reviews and/or recommends safety specifications for new equipment, processes, and materials.
  • Report all incidents immediately to management, and investigate all incidents fully, and advise management on how to prevent similar incidents in the future.
  • Review all recorded injuries (human impact, first aid, property damage, and near misses) with supervisors and make appropriate corrective actions.

Mechanical Engineer

I worked as a Mechanical Engineer at KEC Myanmar from ( February.2021 - Present ). The responsibilities are as follows:

  • Work closely with supplier teams to ensure that all systems, equipment, and components are installed correctly and meet design specifications.
  • Thoroughly review design documents, technical drawings, and schematics of the plant's systems and processes.
  • Coordinate and oversee the execution of various testing activities, including functional tests, performance tests, and safety tests, to ensure systems operate efficiently and safely.
  • Monitor and manage any changes or modifications made during the commissioning process.
  • Provide training to plant operators and maintenance teams on newly commissioned systems and equipment to ensure proper operation and upkeep.
